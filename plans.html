Workout Programs

Beginner Base

Perfect for those just starting out. Focuses on building core strength, mobility, and fundamental movement patterns.

3 Days/Week
Easy

Core Exercises

  • Bodyweight Squats (3x12)
  • Knee Push-ups (3x10)
  • Dumbbell Rows (3x12)
  • Plank (3x30 sec)

Intermediate Build

For those with basic experience looking to pack on muscle and increase endurance. A solid split routine.

4 Days/Week
Medium

Core Exercises

  • Barbell Squats (4x10)
  • Bench Press (4x8)
  • Pull-ups (3xMax)
  • Overhead Press (3x10)

Advanced Shred

High intensity, heavy weights, and advanced training techniques to push past plateaus and maximize results.

6 Days/Week
Hard

  • Weighted Dips (4x8)
  • Bulgarian Split Squats (4x10)

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I miss a workout day?

No stress! Consistency is about the big picture. Simply pick up where you left off the next day. You can easily adjust your weekly review on the dashboard.

Do I need a gym membership?

The Beginner Base plan can be done entirely at home with minimal equipment. The Intermediate and Advanced plans are best performed in a gym setting with access to barbells and machines.

How do I switch plans?

Simply click 'Start Plan' on a different card. Your weekly schedule will automatically update to reflect the new training split.
